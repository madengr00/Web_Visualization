Temperature

What is the weather like as we approach the equator? This website shows anlalysis of data from the OpenWeatherMap API to model weather relationships to Latitude.

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude).

At this time of year (October), the Northern Hemisphere is approaching winter (Fall - usually December 22 to March 20), while the Southern Hemisphere is approaching summer (Springtime!). This might account for the warmer trend in the southern hemisphere. It would be good to combine historical data for the inverse of this situation to balance the analysis. Another possibility for a trend toward the Northern Hemisphere might be that “90% of the human population is in the Northern Hemisphere with the rest residing in the Southern Hemisphere.” https://sciencing.com/differences-between-northern-southern-hemisphere-8260091.html
